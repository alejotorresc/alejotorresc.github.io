Nómada

Client Nómada
Year 2024
Scope Brand Strategy, Naming, Positioning

Nómada came to us without a name, without a story, without a position in the market. They had a product and a conviction — but no language to connect the two. The work began before any visual decision: defining who Nómada would be in the world.

The strategy defined a brand that moves — geographically, culturally, conceptually. The name itself became the first design decision: a word that carries its meaning without needing explanation. Everything that followed was built to honor that restlessness.

Brand strategy isn't a document that lives in a drawer. For Nómada, it became the filter through which every future decision — visual, verbal, experiential — would be measured. The brand now has a compass, not just a logo.
