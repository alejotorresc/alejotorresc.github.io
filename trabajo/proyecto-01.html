Aetnis

Client Aetnis
Year 2025
Scope Strategy, Visual Identity, Communication System

Aetnis needed an identity that could communicate its market position without resorting to the visual codes of its category. The challenge wasn't designing an attractive logo — it was defining a language that could sustain the brand's conversation for years.

The process began with an immersion phase to identify the cultural tensions the brand could inhabit. From there, a conceptual territory was built that informed every visual decision: from typography to photographic tone, to the communication structure at every touchpoint.

The result is an identity system that doesn't depend on an isolated graphic element, but on the coherence between decisions. Every piece of communication reinforces the perception of a brand that thinks before it speaks.
