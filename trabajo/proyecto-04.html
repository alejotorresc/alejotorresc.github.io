Latente

Client Latente
Year 2024
Scope Creative Direction, Art Direction, Campaign

Latente asked for a campaign that could capture what the brand had always felt but never articulated. The brief wasn't about selling a product — it was about making visible something that had been there all along, waiting to be named.

The creative direction established a visual and verbal tone that resists the obvious. Every image, every headline was designed to leave room for the viewer — to suggest rather than declare. The campaign breathes because it doesn't try to say everything at once.

Art direction in this context meant curating an atmosphere, not just composing a frame. The photographs, the typography, the pacing — all calibrated to transmit the same quiet intensity that defines Latente as a brand.
